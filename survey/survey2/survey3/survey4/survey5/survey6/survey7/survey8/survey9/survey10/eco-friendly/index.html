You are Eco-Friendly!

Despite your result here are some examples that you can do in your everyday life to help decrese the amount of (GHG) green house gasses you emit to the environment. You may start by taking the transit instead of your vehicle if possible to work. Not only will it save you in money but it will also decrease the amount of GHG emited as a community. Another example that can help you decrese your GHG emmisions is setting your AC to 75 and above. During a hot day it is possible to set the temperature a little higher when not at home. By doing this, electric bills will be cut short considerably. An AC unit is the main reason that people end up paying a lot of money for electricity.
